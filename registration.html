New Students Apply

We are glad to announce that, from now on whoever wishes to join Muyeye VTC can do so by downloading our registration forms. Students are advised to print the downloaded copies and fill all the required fields.
Filled copies and the required materials as specified in the Registration forms should be brought to our offices during registration.

Muyeye VTC offers both artisan courses and Craft courses, They both have different registration forms, therefore we advise Our new student to check these forms properly before downloading to refrain confusions.
